AgentPhone lets you give AI agents real phone capabilities — provision numbers, send and receive SMS, and handle voice calls with full conversation context. We handle the telecom complexity so you can focus on building your agent.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an agent">
    An agent is the AI persona that owns your phone numbers and handles all interactions — SMS and voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a phone number">
    Provision a real US or CA number and attach it to your agent in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your AI brain">
    Plug in your own LLM via webhook, or use built-in conversation calls — no webhook required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Receive inbound messages and calls. Full conversation history is included in every event payload.
  </Step>
</Steps>
